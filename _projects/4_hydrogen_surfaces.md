Earlier research focused on simulating interactions between gas molecules and material surfaces, in particular **hydrogen dynamics** on [graphene](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graphene) and graphite.

A summary appears in [this Springer chapter](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-642-32955-5_7). A broader list of journal publications is on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=l9E9Zs4AAAAJ&hl=en).
