This work describes a **cloud-based virtual flow metering** system that combines **physical and data-driven** models to monitor **water production** in an **offshore gas** setting—relevant when direct metering is sparse but operational decisions still need reliable rates.

Published in [*Digital Chemical Engineering*](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/digital-chemical-engineering), volume 9, article 100124 (2023): [ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S277250812300042X) · DOI [10.1016/j.dche.2023.100124](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.dche.2023.100124).
