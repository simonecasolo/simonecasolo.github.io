Multiphase flow can be predicted when direct measurements are missing, using physical simulators, machine learning, or **hybrid** combinations—especially valuable with contextualised live data in the cloud. This use case focused on late-life wells with high water cuts, where accurate oil rates have outsized economic impact.

Published in [Digital Chemical Engineering, Vol. 9, 100124 (2023)](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S277250812300042X).
